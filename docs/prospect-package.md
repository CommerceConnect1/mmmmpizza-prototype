## B. Website and SEO audit

Critical finding:
The live homepage contains unrelated casino and gambling links/content. This is the strongest outreach hook because it creates brand trust, SEO quality, and possible security concerns.

High-impact issues:
- Outdated visual presentation
- Fragmented menu navigation
- Weak mobile-first ordering journey
- Location actions are not modernized around call, directions, and order CTAs
- Coupon page relies heavily on images and print-style offers
- Hiring and franchise content exists but is not presented as a modern lead funnel

Business impact:
- Lower customer confidence
- Possible lost orders on mobile
- Weaker local search visibility
- Missed catering and large-order leads
- Brand reputation risk from unrelated homepage content

## C. Outreach positioning

Lead with business risk and customer trust, not design.

Core message:
While reviewing the site, I noticed a few issues that may affect customer trust and local search visibility, including unrelated content appearing on the live website. I also put together recommendations for improving ordering, locations, menu browsing, and catering conversion.
